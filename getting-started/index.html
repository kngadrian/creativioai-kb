Pro Tips for Successâ€‹

Quality Inputs = Quality Outputsâ€‹

  • Product photos: Well-lit, sharp focus, ideally on white/neutral background
  • High resolution: 1000px+ on longest side recommended
  • Clean backgrounds: Use AI Magic Tools to remove busy backgrounds first

Effective Promptsâ€‹

When using text prompts in Create Photoshoot:

  • Be specific: "Modern living room with morning sunlight" vs "nice room"
  • Include mood: "Minimal, peaceful, Scandinavian aesthetic"
  • Mention lighting: "Golden hour", "soft natural light", "dramatic shadows"
  • Reference style: "Magazine-quality", "professional product photography"

Workflow Efficiencyâ€‹

  • Batch generate: Create multiple variations at once
  • Save successful prompts: Keep a doc of prompts that worked well
  • Use Brand Kit: Set it up once, use everywhere
  • Organize with tags: Keep Your Assets library organized
  • Test and iterate: Try multiple styles, learn what converts

Creative Best Practicesâ€‹

  • Less is more: Simple compositions often perform better
  • Consistent branding: Use Brand Kit for color/font consistency
  • Mobile-first: Most viewers are on mobileâ€”keep text large and readable
  • Test variations: Create multiple versions and measure performance
  • Follow platform specs: Match aspect ratios to platform requirements

Common Questionsâ€‹

Q: Do I need design skills to use CreativioAI?
A: No! The AI handles the creative heavy lifting. You just need good product photos and basic creative direction.

Q: What image formats are supported?

Q: Can I use the generated images commercially?

Q: How many images can I generate?

Q: Can I edit AI-generated images?
