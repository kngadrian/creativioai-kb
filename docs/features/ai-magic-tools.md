# AI Magic Tools

**AI Magic Tools** is a suite of 10 powerful, single-purpose image editing tools powered by artificial intelligence. Each tool is designed to handle one specific task quickly and precisely — no design skills required.

![AI Magic Tools Overview](/img/tools/tools-overview.png)

## How It Works

Every tool follows the same simple workflow:

1. **Upload** an image (drag & drop, click to browse, or select from your Asset Library)
2. **Configure** any tool-specific options (prompts, colors, styles)
3. **Click Run** to process
4. **Download** or save the result to your assets

:::info Supported Formats
All tools accept **JPG, PNG, and WebP** images. You can upload up to **10 images** per batch.
:::

## Remove Background {#remove-background}

Instantly remove the background from any image with AI precision. Perfect for product photography, profile pictures, and creating composite images.

![Remove Background](/img/tools/remove-background.png)

**How to use:**
1. Upload your image (or select from Asset Library)
2. Click **Run Remove Background**
3. Download the transparent PNG result

**Best for:**
- E-commerce product photos that need white/transparent backgrounds
- Isolating subjects for marketing materials
- Preparing images for composite designs

**Tips:**
- Works best with clear subject-background contrast
- Complex edges (hair, fur) are handled automatically by the AI
- Output is a transparent PNG — ready for any background

## Magic Remover {#magic-remover}

Erase unwanted objects, people, or defects from your photos. The AI intelligently fills in the removed area to match the surrounding scene.

![Magic Remover](/img/tools/magic-remover.png)

**How to use:**
1. Upload your image
2. In the **"What to remove?"** field, describe what you want erased (e.g., "the person in the background", "the trash can on the left")
3. Click **Run Magic Remover**

**Best for:**
- Removing photobombers from vacation shots
- Cleaning up product photos (removing dust, scratches, labels)
- Erasing watermarks or distracting elements

**Tips:**
- Be specific in your description — "the red car in the background" works better than "car"
- Works best when the object to remove isn't the main subject
- The AI fills in the gap with contextually appropriate content

---

## Text Remover {#text-remover}

Automatically detect and remove text overlays from images. Ideal for cleaning up screenshots, stock photos with watermarks, or images with unwanted captions.

![Text Remover](/img/tools/text-remover.png)

**How to use:**
1. Upload your image
2. Click **Run Text Remover** — no configuration needed
3. The AI detects and removes all visible text

**Best for:**
- Removing watermarks from stock images
- Cleaning up screenshots for presentations
- Removing captions, labels, or overlay text from photos

**Tips:**
- Works on all text types: printed, handwritten, overlay, and embedded
- The background behind the text is reconstructed by the AI
- For selective text removal, combine with Magic Remover instead

## Image Enhancer {#image-enhancer}

Improve image quality, fix lighting issues, and sharpen details instantly. Uses AI to analyze and enhance your photo's overall appearance.

![Image Enhancer](/img/tools/image-enhancer.png)

**How to use:**
1. Upload your image
2. Click **Run Image Enhancer**
3. Compare the enhanced result with your original

**Best for:**
- Improving low-quality product photos
- Fixing underexposed or overexposed images
- Sharpening blurry or soft images
- Quick touch-ups before publishing

**Tips:**
- Works great as a first step before using other tools
- Enhances color balance, sharpness, and dynamic range
- Best results on photos that are slightly soft or underlit

## Image to Text (OCR) {#image-to-text}

Extract text content from images using advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology. Can also answer questions about the image content.

![Image to Text](/img/tools/image-to-text.png)

**How to use:**
1. Upload your image
2. *(Optional)* Enter a **Specific Question** — e.g., "What is the price shown?" or "What brand is this?"
3. Click **Run Image to Text**

**Best for:**
- Extracting text from screenshots, receipts, or documents
- Reading product labels, prices, or serial numbers from photos
- Getting descriptions of image content
- Converting handwritten notes to digital text

**Tips:**
- Leave the question field empty for a full text extraction
- Use a specific question to get targeted information from the image
- Works with printed text, handwritten text, and even stylized fonts

## Search & Replace {#search-and-replace}

Swap objects in your image using simple text prompts. Describe what to find and what to replace it with — the AI handles the rest.

![Search & Replace](/img/tools/search-and-replace.png)

**How to use:**
1. Upload your image
2. In **"Replace this..."** describe the object to find (e.g., "The red apple")
3. In **"With this..."** describe the replacement (e.g., "A green pear")
4. Click **Run Search & Replace**

**Best for:**
- Swapping product colors or variants in marketing photos
- Replacing objects in scene compositions
- Creative experimentation with different elements
- A/B testing product visuals

**Tips:**
- Be descriptive — include color, position, and context for best results
- Works best when the replacement is a similar size/shape to the original
- Great for creating product variations without re-shooting

:::note New Feature
Search & Replace is a recently added tool — keep an eye out for improvements and expanded capabilities.
:::

## Color Changer {#color-changer}

Recolor specific objects or entire scenes effortlessly. Specify what to recolor and the desired new color.

![Color Changer](/img/tools/color-changer.png)

**How to use:**
1. Upload your image
2. In **"Target Object"** describe what to recolor (e.g., "the dress", "the car")
3. In **"New Color"** specify the desired color (e.g., "navy blue", "bright red")
4. Click **Run Color Changer**

**Best for:**
- Creating product color variants from a single photo
- Matching product colors to brand guidelines
- Testing different color schemes for marketing materials
- Changing clothing colors in fashion photography

**Tips:**
- Use specific color names ("coral pink" vs. just "pink") for more accurate results
- Specify the target clearly to avoid unintended color changes
- Works on fabrics, products, backgrounds, and more

![AI Upscaler](/img/tools/ai-upscaler.png)

**How to use:**
1. Upload your image
2. Click **Run AI Upscaler**
