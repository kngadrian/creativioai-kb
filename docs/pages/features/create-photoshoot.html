Create Photoshoot

Purpose: Generate studio-quality product photos using AI by uploading product images and describing the desired scene.

Where to find it

  • Left sidebar → Create Photoshoot
  • URL: /create

Core workflow

  1. Upload product images: drag & drop or browse (supports JPG/PNG/WebP; up to 10 images).
  2. (Optional) Select from Asset Library to reuse previously uploaded assets.
  3. Describe the scene in the Prompt Editor (textbox: “Describe your desired photoshoot scene…”).
  4. Pick a visual style in the Creative Style Engine.
    • Categories observed: Product & E-Commerce, Brand & Marketing, Cinematic & Editorial, Creative & Trending.
    • Example styles shown: Softbox Studio, Golden Hour, Dramatic/Rim, Natural Window, Neon Signs, Hard Flash/Pop, Bokeh (50mm), Wide Angle, Macro Detail, Polaroid/Instant, Film Grain (35mm).
  5. (Optional) Choose Special Days Templates (e.g., seasonal templates).
  6. (Optional) Open Advanced Tweaks (colors, composition, mood).
  7. Set Generation Settings:
    • Aspect Ratio: 1:1, 2:3, 3:2, 3:4, 4:3, 4:5, 5:4, 9:16, 16:9, 21:9
    • Image Quality: Standard (1K), High (2K), Ultra (4K)
    • Negative prompt field (example placeholder: “e.g. blurry, distorted, low quality”)
  8. Click Generate Photoshoot (button is disabled until required inputs are provided).

Tips

  • If the Generate button is disabled, double-check you uploaded at least one product image and wrote a prompt.
  • Use negative prompts to reduce common artifacts (blurry, distorted, low quality, etc.).
