Animation Studio

Animation Studio transforms your static images and text prompts into stunning video animations. Whether you need a product reveal, a social media clip, or an artistic motion piece, Animation Studio offers 5 generation modes and 30 cinematic styles to bring your visuals to life.

Generation Modes‚Äã

Animation Studio offers five distinct modes, each designed for a different creative workflow.

üé¨ Text to Video‚Äã

Generate a video entirely from a text description ‚Äî no source image needed.

How to use:

  1. Select the Text to Video tab (marked "No Image")
  2. Choose a Video Style from the dropdown (or use "None" for prompt-only)
  3. Write your Animation Prompt describing the scene and motion
  4. (Optional) Add a Negative Prompt to exclude unwanted elements
  5. Select Aspect Ratio and Resolution
  6. Click Generate Video

Best for:

  • Concept videos when you don't have a source image
  • Abstract or artistic animations
  • Quick social media content

üñºÔ∏è Image to Video‚Äã

Animate a static image ‚Äî the most popular mode. Upload a product photo or any image and watch it come alive.

How to use:

  1. Select the Image to Video tab (marked "Static Image")
  2. Upload an image (drag & drop or click to browse)
  3. Choose a Video Style (30 options available)
  4. Write an Animation Prompt describing the desired motion
    • Example: "Slow pan across the product with soft ambient lighting, particles floating in the air"
  5. Set aspect ratio and resolution
  6. Click Generate Video

Best for:

  • Product reveal animations for e-commerce
  • Animating photoshoot results
  • Creating social media reels from still photos
  • Marketing hero videos

üîÑ Transition‚Äã

Create smooth video transitions between a first frame and a last frame. The AI generates the motion between the two images.

How to use:

  1. Select the Transition tab (marked "First & Last")
  2. Upload a First Frame image
  3. Upload a Last Frame image
  4. Write a prompt describing the transition motion
  5. Configure style, aspect ratio, and resolution
  6. Click Generate Video

Best for:

  • Before/after reveal videos
  • Product transformation sequences
  • Morph effects between two states
  • Creative transitions for presentations

üé® Style Reference‚Äã

Use up to 3 reference images to guide the visual style of your animation. The AI captures the look and feel of your references.

How to use:

  1. Select the Style Ref tab (marked "3 Images Mix")
  2. Click Add Ref to upload 1‚Äì3 reference images
  3. Write your animation prompt
  4. Choose a video style and settings
  5. Click Generate Video

Best for:

  • Maintaining brand visual consistency
  • Recreating a specific aesthetic from examples
  • Combining multiple visual inspirations
  • Mood board‚Äìdriven video creation

‚è© Extend Video (Longer Clips)‚Äã

Extend an existing generated video to create longer clips. Build on previous animations without starting from scratch.

How to use:

  1. Select the Longer Clips tab (marked "Extend Video")
  2. Select a Source Video from your previously generated animations
  3. Write a continuation prompt
  4. Configure style and settings
  5. Click Generate Video
Prerequisite

You need at least one previously generated video to use this mode. Generate a video first using any other mode.

Best for:

  • Creating longer-form content from short clips
  • Building multi-scene narratives
  • Extending product demos with additional angles

Animation Prompt Tips‚Äã

Writing effective animation prompts is key to getting great results. Here are some tips:

‚úÖ Do‚Äã

  • Describe the motion: "Slow dolly forward toward the product"
  • Set the mood: "Warm golden hour lighting with soft lens flare"
  • Mention camera work: "Orbiting 360¬∞ shot around the box"
  • Include environment details: "On a marble table with city skyline in background"

‚ùå Avoid‚Äã

  • Vague prompts: "Make it look nice"
  • Contradictory instructions: "Fast zoom in while staying still"
  • Excessive detail that conflicts with the chosen style

Example Prompts‚Äã

Product Reveal:

"Slow cinematic push-in on a software box sitting on a reflective dark surface. Volumetric light beams from above, subtle particle dust floating in the air, dramatic reveal feeling."

Social Media Reel:

"Dynamic 360-degree orbit around the product, colorful gradient background transitioning from purple to teal, energetic motion with quick zoom at the end."

Lifestyle Scene:

"Camera slowly pans across a creative workspace, product box centered on desk next to laptop and coffee, morning sunlight streaming through window, cozy productive atmosphere."

Negative Prompts‚Äã

Use the Negative Prompt field to exclude unwanted elements from your animation:

  • blurry, distorted, low quality ‚Äî General quality safeguard
  • text, watermark, logo ‚Äî Prevent unwanted text overlays
  • dark, underexposed ‚Äî Keep the lighting bright
  • shaky, jittery ‚Äî Ensure smooth camera movement

Workflow Tips‚Äã

  1. Start with Fast mode to test your prompt and style, then switch to Quality for the final render
  2. Use Image to Video with your best photoshoot result for product animations
  3. Combine with AI Magic Tools ‚Äî enhance or upscale an image before animating it
  4. Use Extend Video to build longer sequences from short clips you like
  5. Save to Assets ‚Äî all generated videos are automatically saved to Your Assets
