Agency Dashboard

The Agency Dashboard is your central hub for managing clients, team members, and streamlining your agency workflow. Access it from the sidebar or navigate to /agency.

Overview​

The Agency Dashboard helps you:

  • Manage clients - Add, organize, and track all your client accounts
  • Coordinate teams - Manage team member seats and permissions
  • Streamline communication - Use email templates for consistent client outreach
  • Track projects - Keep all client work organized in one place

Clients Management​

The Clients tab is your CRM for managing all client relationships and projects.

Adding a New Client​

  1. Click the "+ Add Client" or "New Client" button
  2. Fill in client details:
    • Client name
    • Email address
    • Company name (optional)
    • Project details
  3. Click Save to create the client profile

Client Dashboard Features​

For each client, you can:

  • View Projects - See all active and completed projects
  • Access Assets - Quickly find client-specific creative assets
  • Track Activity - Monitor project status and recent updates
  • Manage Permissions - Control what clients can see and edit
  • Send Updates - Use email templates to communicate progress

Organizing Clients​

  • Search - Quickly find clients by name or email
  • Filter - Sort by active/inactive, project status, or date added
  • Tags - Add custom tags to categorize clients by industry, priority, etc.

Best Practices​

  • Add clients before starting projects to keep work organized
  • Use consistent naming conventions for easy searching
  • Update client status as projects progress
  • Archive completed clients to keep your active list focused

Team Members​

Manage your team's access to CreativioAI and collaborate efficiently.

Adding Team Members​

  1. Navigate to the Team Members tab
  2. Click "+ Add Team Member" or "Invite Member"
  3. Enter their email address
  4. Select their role and permissions:
    • Admin - Full access to all features and settings
    • Editor - Can create and edit projects, limited settings access
    • Viewer - Can view projects but not edit
  5. Click Send Invitation

The team member will receive an email invitation to join your workspace.

Managing Seats​

Your CreativioAI plan includes a specific number of team member seats:

  • View available vs. used seats at the top of the Team Members page
  • Upgrade your plan for additional seats
  • Remove inactive members to free up seats

Permissions & Roles​

Admin:

  • Full access to Agency Dashboard
  • Can manage billing and subscription
  • Can add/remove team members
  • Can access all clients and projects

Editor:

  • Can create photoshoots and use all creative tools
  • Can access assigned clients
  • Cannot manage team members or billing

Viewer:

  • Can view and download assets
  • Cannot create or edit projects
  • Cannot access client management

Team Collaboration Tips​

  • Assign specific clients to team members to prevent overlap
  • Use consistent naming conventions for projects
  • Leverage Brand Kit to ensure brand consistency across team
  • Regularly review team member access and remove inactive users

Email Templates​

Streamline client communication with pre-built email templates (if available in your plan).

Using Email Templates​

Email templates help you:

  • Save time - Pre-written messages for common scenarios
  • Stay consistent - Ensure brand voice across all communications
  • Onboard clients - Welcome emails and getting started guides
  • Share deliverables - Professional asset delivery templates
  • Request feedback - Structured review and approval requests

Common Template Types​

  • Welcome Email - Introduce new clients to your workflow
  • Project Kickoff - Set expectations and gather requirements
  • Asset Delivery - Share completed creatives professionally
  • Feedback Request - Ask for client input on drafts
  • Project Complete - Wrap up and request testimonials

Customizing Templates​

  1. Select a template from the list
  2. Click Edit or Customize
  3. Modify the content to match your brand voice
  4. Use variables like {client_name} for personalization
  5. Save your customized version

Workflow Integration​

The Agency Dashboard integrates seamlessly with your creative workflow:

Client → Project Workflow​

  1. Add client in Agency Dashboard
  2. Create photoshoot or project linked to that client
  3. Use Brand Kit to apply client's brand assets
  4. Generate assets using AI tools
  5. Deliver via email using templates
  6. Track in dashboard to monitor progress

Cross-Platform Connections​

  • Brand Kit Integration - Apply client brands instantly to projects
  • Asset Library - Client assets automatically organized
  • Create Photoshoot - Start projects directly from client profiles
  • Email Templates - Send updates without leaving the platform

Tips for Agency Users​

Organization​

  • Name clients consistently - Use "Company Name - Project Type" format
  • Use tags - Create tags like "Active", "Pending Review", "Completed"
  • Archive regularly - Move finished projects to keep dashboard clean

Efficiency​

  • Batch client setup - Add all clients at once when onboarding
  • Template everything - Create email templates for every client touchpoint
  • Assign ownership - Give team members specific client responsibilities

Growth​

  • Track metrics - Monitor how many projects per client
  • Client feedback - Use templates to systematically gather testimonials
  • Scale with seats - Add team members as your agency grows

Next Steps​

  • Export and deliver final creatives

Common Questions​

Q: How many clients can I add?
A: There's no limit to the number of clients you can manage.

Q: Can clients access the platform directly?
A: Client access depends on your plan. You can invite clients as "Viewer" role to review and approve assets.

Q: What happens when I remove a team member?
A: Their account is deactivated and the seat becomes available. Their projects and assets remain in your workspace.

Q: Can I transfer a client to another team member?
A: Yes, you can reassign client ownership from the client details page.
