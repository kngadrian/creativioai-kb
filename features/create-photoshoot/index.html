Step-by-step tutorialâ€‹

1) Upload your product imagesâ€‹

  • Drag & drop into the upload area ("Drop product images here")
  • Or click to browse
  • You can also use Select from Asset Library to reuse existing assets

Notes:

  • Supports JPG, PNG, WebP
  • UI indicates Max 10 images

2) Describe the scene (Prompt Editor)â€‹

In Prompt Editor, write what you want the final photo to look like.

Good prompt template:

  • Environment: "on a marble kitchen counter"
  • Lighting: "soft natural window light"
  • Camera: "50mm product shot, shallow depth of field"
  • Mood/branding: "clean, premium, ecommerce-ready"

Example:

Studio product photo of the item on a white seamless backdrop, softbox lighting, crisp shadows, ultra-clean ecommerce look.
