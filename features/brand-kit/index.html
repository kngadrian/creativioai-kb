Brand Kit

Brand Kit lets you define your brand's visual identity in one place. Once configured, CreativioAI uses your brand settings to ensure every AI-generated image and video stays consistent with your brand guidelines.

Why It Matters

Without a Brand Kit, every generation starts from scratch. With one, the AI knows your colors, your style, your voice ‚Äî and every output feels like yours.

Sections‚Äã

Brand Kit is organized into four sections, each controlling a different aspect of your visual identity.

The foundation of your brand ‚Äî who you are and how you communicate.

FieldDescriptionExample
Brand NameYour company or product nameCreativio AI
WebsiteYour brand's website URLcreativio.ai
Brand Description / VoiceDescribe your brand's personality, tone, and target audience"Modern, bold, creative-focused. Targets digital marketers and e-commerce brands. Tone is professional yet approachable."

How it's used:

  • The AI references your brand name and voice when generating creative content
  • Your description helps shape the mood and style of generated images
  • Particularly useful when using Prompt Editor or Magic Mode in Create Photoshoot

Tips:

  • Be specific about your tone: "playful and youthful" vs. "luxury and sophisticated" produces very different results
  • Mention your target audience ‚Äî it helps the AI choose appropriate scenes and aesthetics
  • Include key adjectives that define your brand personality

üé® Color Palette‚Äã

Define your brand colors so the AI can match them in generated content.

How to use:

  1. Your palette starts with default colors (you can modify or remove them)
  2. Click Add Color to add a new color to your palette
  3. Use the color picker or enter a hex code directly (e.g., #7371FC)
  4. Add as many colors as your brand requires

Default palette includes:

  • A primary accent color
  • A secondary/highlight color
  • A dark/background color

How colors are used:

  • When "Use Brand Kit?" is enabled in Create Photoshoot, the AI incorporates your brand colors into backgrounds, accents, and lighting
  • Color Changer tool can reference your palette for consistent recoloring
  • AI Magic Studio respects your color preferences when generating compositions

Tips:

  • Include at least 3 colors: primary, secondary, and a neutral/dark
  • Add your exact hex values from your brand guidelines
  • The AI uses these as guidance ‚Äî subtle influence rather than strict color matching

Using Brand Kit in Other Tools‚Äã

Once your Brand Kit is configured, you can activate it across CreativioAI:

In Create Photoshoot‚Äã

Toggle "Use Brand Kit?" in the Generation Settings panel. When enabled:

  • Generated backgrounds and scenes incorporate your brand colors
  • Logo is automatically placed at your default position
  • The AI considers your brand voice when interpreting prompts

In AI Magic Studio‚Äã

Your brand settings inform the AI's creative decisions when compositing and editing.

In Animation Studio‚Äã

Brand colors and style influence the mood and visual treatment of generated videos.

Best Practices‚Äã

  1. Fill out everything ‚Äî The more the AI knows about your brand, the better the results
  2. Upload a high-quality logo ‚Äî SVG is ideal; PNG should be at least 500√ó500px with transparency
  3. Be descriptive in your Brand Voice ‚Äî Don't just write "professional"; write "Modern, bold, tech-forward. We speak with confidence and clarity. Our audience is creative professionals aged 25-45."
  4. Use exact hex codes ‚Äî Don't approximate; pull colors directly from your brand guidelines
  5. Save after every change ‚Äî Click Save Changes at the top to persist your settings
  6. Review periodically ‚Äî As your brand evolves, update the kit to keep AI outputs aligned

Saving Your Brand Kit‚Äã

After making changes to any section:

  1. Click the Save Changes button at the top of the page
  2. Your settings are saved and immediately available across all tools
  3. Any future generation with "Use Brand Kit?" enabled will use your updated settings
Don't Forget to Save

Changes are not auto-saved. Always click Save Changes before leaving the page, or your updates will be lost.
